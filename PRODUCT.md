# Product

## Register

brand

Mixed surface. Marketing and content pages (/, /about-us, /data-insights, /community, /podcast) use brand register. App surfaces (forum, careers, directory, e-learning) use product register. Default is brand; override per task.

## Users

**Primary:** Practicing pharmacists in Africa. Using PharmaLink to stay connected to the sector, access CPD and e-learning, and participate in professional discourse. Context: mobile-first, variable bandwidth, professional identity is core.

**Secondary:** International donors, NGO partners, government health officials. Using PharmaLink to evaluate programme impact, find evidence of outcomes, and understand the network's reach.

## Product Purpose

PharmaLink is a professional network and knowledge platform for the global pharmaceutical community, anchored in Africa. It exists to connect practitioners, surface evidence of impact, and raise the credibility and visibility of pharmacy as a profession. Success looks like practicing pharmacists treating it as their go-to professional resource, and funders treating it as proof of programme effectiveness.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative · Evidence-led · Mission-driven

Voice: Direct, data-confident, warm without being casual. Speaks from expertise without condescension. Plain language for complex outcomes. Treats the audience as professionals. Technical things made approachable without dumbing down.

Reference: Stripe's precision — clearly defined, pleasurable to interact with, keeps users engaged. Applied to a forward-looking health NGO context: rigorous but not sterile, mission-driven but not preachy.

## Anti-references

- **WebMD**: clinical white, sterile, content-heavy without hierarchy. PharmaLink is not a patient portal.
- **Clinical white aesthetic**: the default "healthcare = white + teal" first-order reflex. Avoid.
- **Corporate-jargon-heavy copy**: "leverage synergies", "robust solutions", "world-class". Every word earns its place.

## Design Principles

1. **Credibility is visual.** Data, evidence, and outcomes must be immediately legible. If the numbers are not prominent, the mission is not landed.
2. **Show, don't announce.** Don't tell users this is trusted. Show the evidence: real dashboards, real people, real programme data.
3. **Mission without martyrdom.** Serious purpose does not mean sombre design. Energy, confidence, and momentum belong here.
4. **Africa is not a footnote.** Geographic and cultural context is central. Design choices should reflect a global-south perspective, not a Western aid-sector aesthetic.
5. **Technical but pleasurable.** Every surface should be clearly defined and delightful to interact with. Precision and warmth are not opposites.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Reduced motion: respect prefers-reduced-motion. Color contrast: AA for all body text. Mobile-first: primary audience is on mobile in lower-bandwidth contexts. No reliance on color alone to convey meaning.
